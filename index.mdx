## Why MindrianOS

A raw AI is brilliant for one turn and forgets the next. MindrianOS adds the four things a raw model structurally cannot be: it **remembers** (your venture persists across every session), it **knows when** (which framework at which stage), it **pushes back** (blocks you when evidence is thin), and it **keeps your data yours** (nothing leaves your machine).

## Get Running in Three Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install with npm">
    Run one command in your terminal. No setup wizard, no config file to write.
    ```bash
    npx @mindrian_os/cli
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart Claude Code">
    Larry starts talking on the next session. The hooks, commands, and Data Room are ready immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Talk — don't type commands">
    Describe what you are working on. Larry routes you to the right framework and files what you say into your room.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  MindrianOS requires a paid Claude plan (Claude Pro, $20/mo, or higher) because it runs on top of Claude. The plugin itself is free.
</Note>
